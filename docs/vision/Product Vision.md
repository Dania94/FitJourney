 Product Vision Document

 Project Name

FitJourney 

 Vision Statement

FitTrack is an all-in-one health and fitness platform designed to help people build and maintain a healthy lifestyle. The application enables users to track their nutrition, fitness activities, and body progress in a single place while providing personalized recommendations tailored to their goals and preferences.

 Problem Statement

People interested in health and fitness often need to use multiple applications to manage different aspects of their wellness journey:

Aspect of Wellness

Calorie and macro tracking

Meal planning

Workout planning

Body measurements

Menstrual cycle tracking

Pregnancy tracking

This fragmented experience makes it difficult to maintain long-term healthy habits and get a complete view of their health and fitness progress.

 Solution Statement

FitTrack provides a single platform that combines:

Feature

Nutrition tracking

Meal planning

Workout planning and tracking

Body measurements and progress tracking

Women's health features

Personalized recommendations

The application adapts to each user's profile, goals, dietary preferences, and favorite cuisines.
